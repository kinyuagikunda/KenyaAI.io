Strengthening Kenya’s AI Ecosystem

This Project is an initiative aimed at building a responsible and inclusive AI ecosystem in Kenya through ethics, governance, and language technology.

Project Focus Areas

  • AI Ethics: Developing an ethics framework for fairness, inclusivity, and human rights protection.
  • AI Governance: Establishing policies and guidelines for AI transparency and risk management.
  • Explainable AI (XAI): Ensuring AI models are interpretable and ethical.
  • Language Technology: Developing a Dholuo-Swahili translation model for low-resourced languages.
  • Knowledge Sharing: Engaging stakeholders through workshops, platforms, and research publications.

Our Goal

To create a sustainable AI ecosystem that serves Kenya’s diverse communities while maintaining ethical AI practices.
